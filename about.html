About Us

Our Story

From a frustrating bike breakdown to a movement that's changing how India maintains its bikes.

Discover Our Mission
2019 - The Beginning

Born from Real Frustration

It was a hot summer afternoon in Bangalore when our founder, Vikram, stood stranded on MG Road with a dead battery. The nearest mechanic was 5 km away. He had to call a friend, wait for 2 hours, and still pay for towing.

That experience got him thinking - why should bike owners suffer like this? Why isn't there a service that comes to you, like a doctor for your bike?

GearUp was born from that simple idea: What if mechanics could come to you? Within 6 months, we had served 500 customers. Within 2 years, we expanded to 10 cities. Today, we've helped over 50,000 riders get back on the road.

One idea.
Infinite possibilities.

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us every single day

Our Mission

To make bike maintenance effortless and accessible. Every rider deserves a reliable service that comes to them, saves them time, and charges them fairly. We're building the future of bike care in India.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted mobile bike service, present in every city and town. We envision a world where no rider is ever stranded because finding a good mechanic was too hard.

Our Values

Customer first. Transparency always. Fair pricing. Skilled hands. We treat every bike like it's our own. These aren't just words - they're how we operate every single day.
